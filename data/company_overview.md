# NimbusFlow - Company Overview

## About Us

NimbusFlow is a cloud-based project management and team collaboration platform founded in 2019 in Austin, Texas. We serve over 15,000 businesses worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, helping teams organize work, track progress, and collaborate seamlessly.

## Our Mission

To simplify work management so teams can focus on what matters most - delivering exceptional results.
