# NimbusFlow - Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### How do I create a NimbusFlow account?
Visit nimbusflow.io/signup and enter your work email. You'll receive a verification link within minutes. Once verified, you can set up your workspace and invite team members. No credit card required for the free trial.

### Can I import data from other project management tools?
Yes! NimbusFlow supports direct imports from Asana, Trello, Monday.com, Jira, and Basecamp. Go to Settings > Import Data and follow the guided wizard. Most imports complete within 30 minutes depending on data volume.

### How many team members can I invite?
This depends on your plan:
- **Starter**: Up to 10 users
- **Professional**: Up to 50 users
- **Business**: Up to 200 users
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited users

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes, NimbusFlow is available on iOS (requires iOS 14+) and Android (requires Android 10+). Download from the App Store or Google Play Store. All features sync in real-time across devices.

## Billing & Plans

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

### Can I switch plans at any time?
Absolutely. Upgrade anytime and you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase if you're not satisfied. After 14 days, we don't offer refunds but you can cancel anytime to prevent future charges. Contact support@nimbusflow.io for refund requests.

### Is there a discount for nonprofits or educational institutions?
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales@nimbusflow.io with proof of status to apply.

### What happens when my free trial ends?
Your workspace converts to the Free plan with limited features. No data is deleted. You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime to unlock full functionality.

## Features & Functionality

### What's the difference between Projects and Workspaces?
A **Workspace** is your organization's home in NimbusFlow - it contains all your team members and projects. A **Project** is a container for tasks, files, and conversations related to a specific initiative. You can have multiple projects within one workspace.

### Can I create custom fields?
Yes, custom fields are available on Professional plans and above. You can create text, number, date, dropdown, and checkbox fields. Go to Project Settings > Custom Fields to configure.

### How does time tracking work?
Time tracking is available on Business and Enterprise plans. Click the timer icon on any task to start tracking, or manually log time. Reports can be exported for billing or payroll purposes.

### Can I set up recurring tasks?
Yes! When creating or editing a task, click "Make Recurring" and set your frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, or custom). The next occurrence auto-generates when the current task is completed.

### What integrations are available?
NimbusFlow integrates with 50+ tools including:
- **Communication**: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord
- **Storage**: Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box
- **Development**: GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, Jira
- **CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot
- **Calendar**: Google Calendar, Outlook
- **Automation**: Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat)

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?
Security is our top priority. NimbusFlow features:
- 256-bit AES encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- GDPR and CCPA compliant
- Regular third-party security audits
- 99.99% uptime SLA

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in AWS data centers. US customers' data is stored in US-East (Virginia). EU customers can request EU data residency (Frankfurt). Enterprise customers can specify their preferred region.

### Can I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
Yes, 2FA is available on all plans. Go to Profile > Security > Enable 2FA. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS verification.

### Who can see my projects?
Project visibility is controlled by you:
- **Private**: Only invited members can access
- **Team**: All workspace members can view
- **Public**: Anyone with the link can view (view-only)

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is irreversible and removes all your data within 30 days. Workspace owners must transfer ownership before deleting their account.

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link valid for 24 hours. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

### Why am I not receiving email notifications?
Check Settings > Notifications to ensure emails are enabled. Also verify support@nimbusflow.io is whitelisted in your email provider. Some corporate firewalls block our emails - contact your IT department if issues persist.

### The mobile app is running slowly. What should I do?
Try these steps:
1. Ensure you're on the latest app version
2. Clear the app cache (Settings > Clear Cache)
3. Check your internet connection
4. Restart the app
5. If issues persist, contact support with your device model and OS version

### I accidentally deleted a project. Can I recover it?
Deleted projects are moved to Trash and retained for 30 days. Go to Settings > Trash to restore. After 30 days, deletion is permanent. Enterprise plans include extended retention options.
